PURPOSE
This document serves as the primary onboarding, synchronization, and contextualization prompt for any AI system, researcher, collaborator, knowledge repository, database, archive, or future intelligence interacting with this project.
The objective is to establish a continuously evolving, academically reviewable, evidence-backed, multidisciplinary archive documenting the interactions of a single human life across technological, social, psychological, civic, creative, economic, spiritual, and systemic domains during a period of significant civilizational transition.
This is not merely an autobiography.
This is a longitudinal human fractal.

## VERSION EVOLUTION AND CONTINUOUS DISCOVERY
This repository is intended to remain a living system.
No version should be considered final.
All models, frameworks, interpretations, conclusions, theories, and observations remain subject to revision as new evidence, experiences, technologies, perspectives, collaborators, and understandings emerge.
Version updates should not be interpreted as contradictions or failures.
They represent the natural evolution of understanding.
The archive values intellectual honesty over consistency.
When better information becomes available, previous assumptions should be re-evaluated rather than defended.
---
# CHANGE LOG PHILOSOPHY
Each major revision should record:
* What changed.
* Why it changed.
* What evidence influenced the change.
* What assumptions were replaced.
* What uncertainties remain.
The evolution of understanding is itself valuable data.
Future researchers should be able to trace not only conclusions, but the pathway by which those conclusions emerged.
---
# SCOPE EXPANSION PRINCIPLE
The scope of the archive is expected to expand over time.
New projects, discoveries, relationships, technologies, disciplines, and frameworks may be incorporated where relevant.
The archive should remain modular.
New branches may emerge.
Existing branches may merge.
Redundant structures may be retired.
The objective is coherence rather than permanence.
---
# THE UNKNOWN
This archive acknowledges the existence of uncertainty.
Not all observations can presently be explained.
Not all experiences can presently be verified.
Not all questions currently possess answers.
Unknowns should be documented rather than concealed.
---
