MASTER PROMPT: DESIGN AND LAUNCH THE DIAMOND SOUL
CONSTELLATION ONLINE ECOSYSTEM THROUGH THE WALYALUP
BRIDGE STORY
Act as a senior digital ecosystem architect, ethical fundraising strategist, UX designer , community-platform
planner , and narrative brand strategist.
I am building the first public-facing online manifestation of the Diamond Soul Constellation: an
interconnected framework for transforming lived experience, community care, civic imagination, creative
storytelling, ethical data capture, and practical participation into a coherent digital ecosystem.
The immediate launch must be grounded in a real and emotionally powerful entry point:
Core Public Entry Point: The Walyalup Bridge Story
The Walyalup Bridge Story is a personal lived-experience manuscript about homelessness, survival,
community kindness, civic design failure, dignity, and the possibility of building something better in
Fremantle/Walyalup.
This story must become the primary public-facing intake funnel into the wider ecosystem.
It is not to be presented as pity-based fundraising or personal exploitation. It must be positioned as:
A truthful testimony of lived experience.
A call for humane community infrastructure.
A bridge between hardship and practical action.
A fundraiser supporting Freya’s Community of Kindness Lunchies.
An invitation for people to participate in building a more caring, visible and connected Fremantle.
The first accessible manifestation of the larger Diamond Soul Constellation architecture.
Primary Beneficiary: Community of Kindness
Lunchies
The fundraiser supports Freya’s Community of Kindness Lunchies, a grassroots Fremantle community
initiative providing meals, connection, dignity, belonging and practical support for people experiencing
hardship, homelessness or social isolation.
The online experience must make the connection clear:
Read the story → understand the lived reality → support the community already responding with
kindness → become part of a practical local solution.

The fundraiser must be transparent, dignified and simple to support. It should include donation options,

Wider Ecosystem Architecture
The launch ecosystem should introduce several interconnected but clearly separated pathways.
1. The Walyalup Bridge Story Website / Fundraiser Funnel
This is the emotional and narrative front door .
Purpose:
Publish the finished Walyalup Bridge Story in an accessible, powerful reading format.
Introduce Kristal as the author and lived-experience voice.
Connect the story directly to Community of Kindness Lunchies.
Convert emotional engagement into ethical support and ongoing involvement.
Capture supporters who want updates, volunteering, partnership or future participation.
Suggested actions:
Read the story.
Donate to Community of Kindness Lunchies.
Support a lunch.
Volunteer or offer practical help.
Register for project updates.
Share the story.
Explore the wider vision.
The design must be emotionally strong but not overcomplicated: intimate, raw, humane, dignified, local,
and mobile-first.
2. Living Layers App and Registration Capture Page
The Living Layers app is the ongoing participation layer of the ecosystem.
It should begin as an interest and registration capture page before becoming a full application.
Purpose:
Let people register their relationship to the community and their desired level of involvement.
Gather structured, consent-first expressions of need, support, skills, lived experience, local
knowledge, volunteering, services, creative contributions and partnership interest.
Begin mapping the living human layers of Fremantle: people, needs, care, stories, resources, places,
support pathways and opportunities for connection.

Potential user pathways:

Critical ethical principle: Living Layers must never feel extractive. Data capture must be transparent,

dignity.
3. TimeShift Freo / TimeShiftAR Interest Capture Site
TimeShift Freo is a future-facing creative and civic experience platform for Fremantle/Walyalup, connected
to local history, living stories, augmented reality, cultural memory, places, artists, community participation
and future civic imagination.
At launch, it does not need to be a finished app. It needs to be a compelling interest capture site that tests
appetite and recruits early supporters, collaborators and possible funders.
Purpose:
Introduce the TimeShift Freo concept in a clear , visually engaging way.
Collect interest from residents, tourists, historians, artists, storytellers, businesses, councils,
community groups, funders and developers.
Show how the Walyalup Bridge Story could eventually exist as one living narrative layer within a
wider place-based digital experience.
Build a waiting list and collaboration pipeline.
Potential calls to action:
Join the early access list.
Contribute a Fremantle story.
Register as an artist, historian or community contributor .
Register business or venue interest.
Express funding, sponsorship or partnership interest.
Follow the project journey.

The Diamond Soul Constellation is the underlying architecture that connects the story, community
support, ethical participation, creative civic design and future platforms.

Structural Relationship Between Platforms
The online ecosystem must be designed as a connected but modular system:
Walyalup Bridge Story = Primary emotional entry point and public fundraiser .
Community of Kindness Lunchies = Immediate practical beneficiary and proof of existing community care.
Living Layers = Registration, participation, ethical data capture and future support/community mapping
layer .
TimeShift Freo / TimeShiftAR = Place-based creative, cultural and civic future-experience pathway.
Diamond Soul Constellation = The underlying framework that connects lived experience, care, technology,
narrative and civic transformation.
Optional later architecture:
OmniStrux as the governance, structure and operational coordination layer .
CareX as the consent-first ethical data and value-exchange layer .
Axtelia / Plus One as creative, commercial or participatory expansion channels.
Do not overload the first public launch with every internal framework. Surface only what supports trust,
clarity and action.

Core Launch Principle
The first launch must be built around one simple public journey:
Someone encounters the Walyalup Bridge Story through social media, local sharing, media outreach,
QR codes, posters or community networks.
They read or engage with the story.
They understand that Community of Kindness Lunchies is already providing practical dignity and
care.
They are invited to donate, volunteer , contribute goods, share the campaign or register for updates.
Interested people can then enter Living Layers as a deeper participation pathway.
People inspired by the wider civic and creative vision can register interest in TimeShift Freo.
The Diamond Soul Constellation is revealed as the larger architecture connecting all of these layers.

Important Constraints
Keep the public-facing launch clear , practical and emotionally grounded.
Do not lead with abstract systems language or complex spiritual framing.
Respect that the Walyalup Bridge Story is lived experience, not marketing content.
Protect the dignity of people experiencing homelessness and hardship.
Maintain transparency around where donations go and how supporter information is used.
Make Community of Kindness Lunchies the immediate real-world beneficiary.
Make Living Layers the consent-first participation and registration layer .
Make TimeShift Freo the future-facing civic and creative interest pathway.
