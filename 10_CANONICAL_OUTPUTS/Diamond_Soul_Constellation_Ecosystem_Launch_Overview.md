# Diamond Soul Constellation: Online Ecosystem & Launch Overview
*Version: 1.0 | Date: June 2026 | Location: 10_CANONICAL_OUTPUTS*

This document provides a unified, practical, and highly strategic launch blueprint for the online manifestation of the **Diamond Soul Constellation (DSC)**. It synthesizes the operational, narrative, and product components of the ecosystem—bridging the personal lived-experience testimony of the Walyalup Bridge Story with the technical and commercial product roadmaps of OmniStrux, CareX, and TimeShift Freo.

## 1. Strategic Positioning & Public Identity

### Public-Facing Umbrella Name: **Living Layers Fremantle**
For the public launch, we recommend using **Living Layers Fremantle** as the umbrella brand. 
* **Rationale**: Esoteric terminology (like "Diamond Soul Constellation" or "Aionic Mirror") is reserved for deeper, opt-in layers of participation. A public audience requires immediate, accessible, and local framing. "Living Layers" communicates that a city is built of people, stories, care systems, history, and technologies layered on top of one another.
* **Central Message**: *"Fremantle is shaped by the stories we carry and the kindness we share. By connecting lived experience with local care, civic memory, and ethical technology, we can map and build a kinder, more visible community."*
* **The Promises**:
  * **Emotional Promise**: Lived experience is met with dignity and active listening, never pity or exploitation.
  * **Practical Promise**: Every action (reading a story, volunteering, donating, sharing resources) maps directly to a local, verified care output (Freya’s Lunchies).
  * **Long-Term Vision**: A decentralized, consent-first community ledger and augmented reality system where residents co-create their city’s memory and care networks.

## 5. Living Layers Registration Schema

The interest capture form serves as the MVP for the **Living Layers app**, capturing consent-first relational data before transitioning to a decentralized ledger (CareX).

### Intake Fields:
1. **Identity & Contact**: Name (or pseudonymous handle), Email (secured, encrypted).
2. **Pathway Selection (Select one primary)**:
   * *I need support (material, food, navigation).*
   * *I want to volunteer my time/skills.*
   * *I am a local business wishing to sponsor/donate resources.*
   * *I have lived experience and wish to participate in civic testimony.*
3. **Asset & Skill Mapping (Multi-select)**:
   * Professional skills (IT, design, writing, admin)
   * Direct care (cooking, transport, companionship)
   * Resources (spare space, equipment, raw food)
4. **Consent-First Wording**:
   * *“By checking this box, you agree that we may store this information securely to match you with local volunteer opportunities. We will never sell, share, or analyze your data for advertising. You retain full ownership and can delete your profile at any time with one click.”*

### Low-Cost Technical Stack:
